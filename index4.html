Coir Crafts

Coir, made from coconut husks, is used to create various handcrafted items such as mats, rugs, and handicrafts. The coir craft industry is an important part of Kerala’s traditional craftsmanship.

Manipuri Classical Dance

One of the major classical dance forms of India, characterized by its soft and graceful movements. It primarily focuses on Ras Leela, which depicts the love between Lord Krishna and Radha

Goan Wooden Craft:

This includes the creation of intricate wooden carvings and sculptures, often depicting religious symbols, animals, and local motifs. The craftsmanship is evident in both functional items and decorative pieces.

Blue Pottery

Blue pottery is known for its distinctive blue and white designs. This form of pottery involves intricate hand-painting and glazing.
